About Me

Radiation Studio

    Hello Travelers! I'm Gamma, the artist behind Radiation Studio. I love cosplay, fandom, and making art in many forms.

    Radiation Studio focuses on making art inspired by anime, pop culture, fantasy themes, and original concepts. In the forms of prints, keychains, stickers, jewelry and more! The goal of Radiation Studio is to create fun art that adds to the fandom experience!
